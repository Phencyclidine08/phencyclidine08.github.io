Staff Bio

Administrator, Policy Architect, and Mod Creator

Allen Scott is a dedicated Administrator and platform advocate with a strong focus on fairness, inclusivity, and thoughtful governance. Passionate about gaming and modding spaces, Allen brings a balanced approach to moderation—protecting creative freedom while upholding community standards that foster respectful, welcoming environments.

Allen’s journey with GameBanana began on May 17, 2011, starting as a passionate community member and contributor. In 2017, Allen became the Game Manager for the Team Fortress 2 section, where he helped cultivate one of the platform’s most active modding hubs. After a year of leadership, Allen stepped into a moderation role, later advancing to Super Moderator just two years later. By 2021, Allen had become a bona fide Administrator, where he now helps steer the direction of the platform as a whole.

Allen frequently plays Devil’s advocate in moderation discussions to guard against unnecessary censorship and protect fair, creative expression, ensuring that the platform’s rules are applied with consistency and an open mind. Whether managing sensitive reports, enforcing content guidelines, or drafting policy-aligned responses, Allen consistently emphasizes clarity, fairness, and nuance.

In addition to platform moderation, Allen is an active contributor to the Team Fortress 2 modding community, regularly creating and sharing mods that enhance the game’s creativity and player expression. Allen understands the modding ecosystem from both a creator’s and a moderator’s perspective, bringing valuable insight to every decision.

Allen has also authored and led the development of major policy documents, including the platform’s AI Generated Content Policy, Multiple Accounts Policy, as well as the complete refinement and integration of the NSFW Policy—now known as the Indecent Content Policy—into the platform’s Content Rating system. These contributions have been instrumental in shaping the site’s evolving standards, ensuring they remain comprehensive, adaptable, and aligned with the needs of the community.

Outside of moderation, Allen enjoys graphic design and competitive gaming, bringing the same level of creativity, strategy, and attention to detail to personal projects and high-level play.
